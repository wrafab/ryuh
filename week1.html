Fusion 360 - CAD

For week 1 in Digital Engineering, we started on a CAD or Computer Aided Design software called Autodesk Fusion 360.
We were given choices on what to make and I decided to make a stamp.
The issue that I first faced when I was trying to make a text stamp was that it was very complicated to mirror image the text on to the stamp itself.
So I decided to go with image tracing, but I realized that I would have to make rectangles instead of lines.
It was very annoying since I traced the whole image out with just lines.
So I just decided to go with a simple stamp that says, "No".
It also has the letter H on the top of the stamp that stands for totally not my name.
After I created the stamp in Fusion 360 and saved it as a STL file, I went onto MakerBot Print.
I then scaled it down and rotated it with supports so that the stamp part would be nice and clean.
