Vacuum Forming and Lamination- Skateboards

Making the Skateboard

I made a CorelDraw file that matched the dimensions of the board.
I decided to laser cut onto my skateboard so I put an image then saved the file.
I got the very bottom boardnad laser cut my image onto it.
Then, I got a partner to help out on gluing all the board together.
I put the glued boards in a bag with a mesh and the curve mold and put tar tape so that so air leaks.
However, the bag leaked and I wasted 20 minutes of my life.
I re-taped it and pumped it so that the edges get curved due to the curve mold.
I took it out and one side of the skateboard was curved and the other was not really.
Then I finished the skateboard off by grinding all the edges and sanding down the top and bottom.
